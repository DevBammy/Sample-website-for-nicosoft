FAQs

What do I need to know?

Where is the live stream taking place?
We work worldwide with remote contributors or you can book one of our studios.
When is the live stream?
Usually, we’d need at least one week’s notice so we can adequately prepare for a live stream. However, certain times of the year are busier than others so don’t leave it too late!
Do you have an AV company looking after any aspects of the production?
If you’re planning on using a hired venue for a webinar or live stream then sound (microphones & in-room PA) will sometimes be provided. However, if you’re planning using one of your own locations we’ll need to allow for sound technicians.
What platform would you like live stream sent to (via social media or private/securely)?
We can distribute your stream to multiple social media platforms and your website simultaneously. If you’re planning an internal comms broadcast, webcast or webinar we'll make it secure for you. If you have a contract with a webinar or virtual event platform we’re happy to plug our production in your account.
